---
title: "Importul subcontractorilor"
description: "Incarca in masa transportatorii subcontractati in Druma."
icon: "file-import"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deschide pagina de import">Mergi la **Expediere -> Subcontractori -> Import** si apasa **Descarca Template**.</Step>
  <Step title="Completeaza CSV-ul">Un subcontractor per rand: denumire companie, numar TVA, tara, email, telefon, note.</Step>
  <Step title="Incarca si revizuieste">Verifica randurile cu erori (de obicei format TVA invalid).</Step>
  <Step title="Confirma importul">Apasa **Importa [X] subcontractori**.</Step>
</Steps>

Druma potriveste subcontractorii existenti dupa numarul de TVA la re-import.

Dupa import, genereaza linkurile de portal pentru fiecare subcontractor: deschide profilul si apasa **Genereaza Link Portal**.
