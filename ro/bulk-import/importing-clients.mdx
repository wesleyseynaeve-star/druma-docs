---
title: "Importul clientilor"
description: "Incarca in masa clientii in Druma folosind un template CSV."
icon: "file-import"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deschide pagina de import">Mergi la **Clienti -> Import** si apasa **Descarca Template**.</Step>
  <Step title="Completeaza CSV-ul">Un client per rand: denumire companie, numar TVA, tara, adresa, nume contact, email contact, telefon contact, termene plata (zile), moneda, note.</Step>
  <Step title="Incarca si revizuieste">Incarca CSV-ul. Randurile rosii au erori (format TVA invalid sau campuri obligatorii lipsa).</Step>
  <Step title="Confirma importul">Apasa **Importa [X] clienti**.</Step>
</Steps>

Clientii fara numar de TVA nu pot primi transmisii e-Factura ANAF. Adauga TVA-ul inainte de generarea primei facturi.

Druma potriveste clientii existenti dupa numarul de TVA la re-import.
