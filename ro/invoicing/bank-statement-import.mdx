---
title: "Import extras de cont bancar"
description: "Cum sa importi extrasele de cont bancar in Druma."
icon: "file-import"
---

Formate suportate: **CAMT.053** (XML European, BCR/BRD/Raiffeisen/UniCredit), **MT940** (SWIFT text).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Descarca extrasul">Descarca in format CAMT.053 sau MT940 din internet banking.</Step>
  <Step title="Mergi la Facturi -> Reconciliere">Modulul de Reconciliere din Druma.</Step>
  <Step title="Apasa Import Extras">Selecteaza fisierul - Druma il parseaza automat.</Step>
  <Step title="Verifica si confirma">Revizuieste tranzactiile si apasa Confirma Importul.</Step>
</Steps>
