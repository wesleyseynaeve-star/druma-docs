---
title: "Brokeri"
description: "Gestioneaza registrul de brokeri de marfa."
icon: "building-columns"
---

Mergi la **Comercial -> Brokeri**. Apasa **Adauga Broker**: denumire, TVA, tara, email, telefon, comision, note.

La crearea unei comenzi de tip **Vanzare capacitate**, selecteaza brokerul din campul Broker.
