---
title: "Taxe reale de taxa"
description: "Inregistreaza taxele de vigneta si taxa platite efectiv."
icon: "road"
---

Soferul inregistreaza in aplicatia mobila: comanda activa -> **Adauga Taxa** -> suma, tara, tip (vigneta/taxa punct).

La generarea facturii, taxele pot fi incluse ca element separat de trecere catre client.
