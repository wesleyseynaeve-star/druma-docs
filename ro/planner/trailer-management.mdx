---
title: "Gestionarea remorcilor"
description: "Gestioneaza remorcile ca active independente."
icon: "truck-ramp-box"
---

Mergi la **Camioane -> Remorci**.

Apasa **Adauga Remorca**: numar inmatriculare, tip, dimensiuni interne, capacitate, volum, LDM maxim.

Druma actualizeaza automat locatia remorcii la fiecare livrare. Atasare la tractor: **Camioane -> Vehicule -> [vehicul] -> Remorca atasata**.
