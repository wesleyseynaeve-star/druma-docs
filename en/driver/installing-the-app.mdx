## What Kind of App Is It?

The Druma driver app is a **PWA (Progressive Web App)**. This means it looks and works like a regular app on your phone — it has an icon on your home screen, works when your signal is weak, and loads fast — but you don't download it from the App Store or Google Play.

Your dispatcher sends you a link. You add it to your home screen. Done.

This is good for you because:
- No waiting for app store approvals or updates
- No storage space wasted
- Works on any modern Android or iOS phone

---

## What You Need Before Installing

Before you install, your dispatcher needs to have:
- Added your phone number to your driver profile in Druma
- Sent you the PWA link for your company's Druma account

If you haven't received a link, contact your dispatcher — they can send it by WhatsApp or SMS.

---

## Installing on Android (Chrome)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chrome">
    Make sure you're using **Google Chrome** — not Samsung Internet, Firefox, or another browser. Chrome is required for Android installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the link your dispatcher sent">
    Tap the link your dispatcher sent you. The Druma driver app will open in Chrome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the browser menu">
    Tap the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add to Home Screen">
    Select **"Add to Home Screen"** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    A dialog box appears with the app name. Tap **"Add"**. The Druma icon will appear on your home screen like any other app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Chrome Add to Home Screen option on Android](/images/placeholder.png)

<Note>
If you don't see "Add to Home Screen" in the menu, look for an **Install App** banner that sometimes appears at the bottom of the screen instead. Tap it to install.
</Note>

## Installing on iOS (Safari)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Safari">
    You **must use Safari** on iPhone/iPad — not Chrome, not Firefox. iOS only allows PWA installation through Safari.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the link your dispatcher sent">
    Tap the link. The Druma driver app opens in Safari.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Share button">
    At the bottom of the screen, tap the **Share** button — it looks like a box with an upward arrow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen">
    In the share sheet that appears, scroll down to find **"Add to Home Screen"** and tap it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add">
    A preview shows the app name and icon. Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner. The icon appears on your home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging In for the First Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app from your home screen">
    Tap the Druma icon you just added.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your phone number">
    Type your mobile number, including the country code (e.g., +40 for Romania).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the SMS code">
    Druma sends a 6-digit verification code to your number by SMS. Enter the code in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your orders and active loads appear immediately. No username or password to remember — your phone number is your login.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Your phone number must match exactly what your dispatcher entered in your driver profile. If the SMS code doesn't arrive or the number isn't recognised, ask your dispatcher to check your profile.
</Warning>

---

## Keeping the App Working

**Android:** Make sure Chrome is allowed to run in the background. Go to **Settings → Apps → Chrome → Battery** and set it to Unrestricted (or turn off battery optimisation for Chrome).

**iOS:** Make sure Safari notifications are enabled if your company uses push notifications. Go to **Settings → Safari → Notifications** and allow them.

**Both:** Keep your phone's operating system reasonably up to date. The app works on any modern smartphone — you don't need the latest model.
