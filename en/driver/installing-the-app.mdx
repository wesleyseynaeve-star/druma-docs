## What Kind of App Is It?

It depends on your phone:

- **Android**: Druma has a native Android app available on **Google Play**. You download and install it like any other app.
- **iPhone / iPad (iOS)**: Druma uses a **PWA (Progressive Web App)**. It looks and works like a regular app — it has an icon on your home screen and works when your signal is weak — but you install it directly from Safari, not the App Store.

---

## What You Need Before Installing

- A smartphone (Android or iPhone/iPad)
- A mobile number or email address to register your account

You register and activate your account the same way as any other Druma user — no special link or token from your dispatcher is needed. Your dispatcher will assign loads to your account once you're set up.

---

## Installing on Android

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Google Play">
    On your Android phone, open the **Google Play Store**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Druma">
    Search for **"Druma"** and tap the app in the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Install">
    Tap **"Install"** and wait for the download to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Tap **"Open"** or find the Druma icon on your home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Make sure Chrome and the Druma app are allowed to run in the background. Go to **Settings → Apps → Druma → Battery** and set it to Unrestricted (or turn off battery optimisation for the app).
</Note>

---

## Installing on iOS (Safari)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Safari">
    You **must use Safari** on iPhone/iPad — not Chrome, not Firefox. iOS only allows PWA installation through Safari.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Druma driver app URL">
    Navigate to the Druma driver app URL provided by your dispatcher or company admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Share button">
    At the bottom of the screen, tap the **Share** button — it looks like a box with an upward arrow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen">
    In the share sheet that appears, scroll down to find **"Add to Home Screen"** and tap it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add">
    A preview shows the app name and icon. Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner. The icon appears on your home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

![iOS Safari Share sheet with Add to Home Screen](/images/placeholder.png)

<Note>
Make sure Safari notifications are enabled if your company uses push notifications. Go to **Settings → Safari → Notifications** and allow them.
</Note>

## Registering and Activating Your Account

You register as a driver the same way any user registers in Druma — there is no special one-time token link required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Tap the Druma icon on your home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Enter your mobile number or email address and follow the on-screen steps to set up your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your account">
    Druma sends a verification code by SMS or email. Enter it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your account is active. Your dispatcher will assign loads to your driver profile once you're registered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Make sure you register with the same phone number or email address your dispatcher has on file for you. If they can't find your account, ask them to check the details in your driver profile.
</Warning>
