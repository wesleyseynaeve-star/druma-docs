SmartBill is a popular Romanian cloud-based invoicing and accounting platform. Druma can push invoices to SmartBill automatically so you don't have to enter them twice.

## Setting up SmartBill

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your SmartBill API key">
    Log into SmartBill → **Cont → Setări → API** → copy your API key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure in Druma">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → SmartBill** → paste your API key → enter your company CIF (Romanian tax ID).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    Click **Test Connection**. Druma verifies the credentials with SmartBill's API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable auto-push">
    Toggle **Auto-push invoices** — enabled by default once connected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How it works

When you generate an invoice in Druma, it's automatically pushed to SmartBill as a **draft invoice** within seconds. You then review and issue it from within SmartBill.

Invoice data pushed includes: client details, line items (transport, surcharges), amounts, VAT, invoice date, due date, and currency.

## Status sync

When you mark an invoice as **Sent** or **Paid** in SmartBill, the status updates in Druma's invoice list accordingly. This keeps your records consistent without manual updates on both sides.

## What SmartBill integration does NOT replace

- **ANAF e-Factura:** Druma submits to ANAF directly (via SPV API) — you do not need SmartBill for e-Factura compliance
- **SAGA/WinMENTOR export:** if your accountant uses SAGA or WinMENTOR, use those exports separately
- **Payment recording:** payments should still be recorded in Druma for accurate overdue management and bank reconciliation

<Note>
SmartBill is best suited for companies that use SmartBill as their primary accounting tool. If your accountant uses SAGA or WinMENTOR, those integrations are more appropriate.
</Note>
